### Printing out Family Trees using topola-viewer

Go to: **[pewu.github.io/topola-viewer](https://pewu.github.io/topola-viewer/)** 

How to use this exact link: 

1. Click the blue **Open file** button on that page.
2. Choose your `.ged` file from Ancestry.
3. Your whole tree will instantly display as a clean, names-only text map. [](https://github.com/PeWu/topola-viewer/blob/master/README.md)
    
Two quick settings to get the WHOLE tree: 

Once your file loads, make two fast adjustments on the left-hand menu panel to make sure nobody is left out: [](https://www.gramps-project.org/wiki/index.php/Addon:Interactive_Family_Tree)

1. Change the **Chart Type** from "Hourglass" to **All Relatives**. This forces the system to pull in every single branch, cousin, and marriage line at once. [](https://github.com/PeWu/topola-viewer/blob/master/README.md)
2. Click **Print** or **Export** on that same left menu, select **PDF**, and choose the **Single Page** layout to save it as one continuous giant map file. [](https://github.com/PeWu/topola-viewer/blob/master/README.md)
